    Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta, otherwise known as Lady Gaga, is an American singer, songwriter and actress. Gaga began performing as a teenager, singing at open mic nights.

  • 1986 - Lady Gaga is born in New York City.
  • 2003 - Gaga gained early admission to Collaborative Arts Project 21 (CAP21), a music school at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts.
  • 2005 - Gaga withdrew from NYU to focus on her music career.
  • 2007 - Gaga was signed to Streamline Records, an imprint of Interscope Records.
  • 2008 - The Fame, Gaga's debut album, was released.
  • 2009 - "Poker Face" was the world's best-selling single of the year, with 9.8 million copies sold.
  • 2011 - Gaga released Born This Way, her second studio album.
  • 2013 - ARTPOP, Gaga's third studio album, was released to mixed reviews.
  • 2015 - Gaga performed a medley of songs from The Sound of Music at the 87th Academy Awards.
  • 2016 - Joanne, Gaga's fifth studio album, was released.
  • 2017 - Gaga was the headlining act during the Super Bowl LI halftime show.
  • 2018 - Gaga portrays Ally in the remake of A Star is Born.
